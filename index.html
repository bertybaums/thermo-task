Thermometer Estimation

Three thermometers are placed in the same pot of water, but they don't all agree. Your job: estimate the actual water temperature based on the three readings.

You'll see a series of scenarios. For each one, look at the three readings on the scale, then drag the slider to your best estimate. There are no trick questions and no time limit.

You can complete as many or as few as you like. Each scenario takes about 10 seconds.
